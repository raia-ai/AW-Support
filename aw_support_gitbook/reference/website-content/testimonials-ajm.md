# AJM - A+W Software GmbH

## AJM okna-vrata-senčila d.o.o.

## Integrated ERP and CIM solution installed

The Slovenian window and door manufacturer AJM in Maribor, Slovenia, offers its customers almost the entire spectrum of windows, doors, conservatories and shade systems. With PVC, wood and aluminium as frame materials. This is possible thanks to a high-performance software solution from A+W Cantor alongside modern machinery.

AJM works with a fully networked A+W Cantor software solution, with CNC machines from Rotox and Homag, and only uses material from leading European manufacturers such as Aluplast, VBH, Winkhaus, Siegenia, Rodenberg, Hella and others. All products are tested by ift Rosenheim, with which AJM works closely. In addition, the company is ISO 9001 certified.

“Thanks to the online ordering, AJM is already saving us eight man-days per month!”, rejoices Daniel Keuc, Head of Development/Informatics, AJM okna-vrata-senčila d.o.o.

# Moving to Fernwald - A+W Software GmbH

by | Jan 11, 2024

![Fernwald-office](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/vlcsnap-2024-06-20-16h44m35s403-scaled-1-1080x675.jpg)

2023 was an important year for A+W, as we relocated our headquarters to Fernwald. The new location in Fernwald not only offers us more modern office space, but also the opportunity to make our services even more efficient and further expand our market presence. The decision to move to Fernwald was carefully planned and implemented to ensure that our employees can benefit from the many advantages. With the move, A+W have set the course for a successful future and look forward to realizing our visions and goals at the new location.

# Part of the Glaston Corporation

by | Jan 11, 2024

![2007_glaston](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/2007_glaston.webp)

In July 2007, ALBAT+WIRSAM Software AG becomes a part of the Finnish Glaston Group.  
Glaston integrates A+W as their business area “Software Solutions”, thus ensuring a high degree of independence for A+W.  
A+W develops the first model of a holistic production and machine control system.

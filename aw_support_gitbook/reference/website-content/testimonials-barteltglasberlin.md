#### "The future is not something you have, it’s something you make!"

#### Kurt Horn

##### \- BarteltGLASBerlin GmbH & Co. KG

## Premium products rather than mass production

BarteltGLASBerlin GmbH & Co. was founded in 1960 by Gerda and Heinz Bartelt in Berlin. Today, the company is headquartered in Berlin-Mariendorf. High-quality flat-glass products are produced on an area of 13.500 m2. The company currently employs 120 members of staff. Its core competencies lie in the area of glass processing, fenestration, safety glass and glass design.

In January 2015, the company’s welcomed its 3rd generation family members to the management board. The children Julia Geburzi-Horn and Robert Horn take over management of the company.

Bartelt & Sohn plans and manages its production connected with the PPS system A+W Production; in the administrative area, the company employs the latest A+W Business generation with integrated order entry – the Bartelt & Sohn team knows that it cannot make efficient use of a high-tech machine park without intelligent networked information technology.

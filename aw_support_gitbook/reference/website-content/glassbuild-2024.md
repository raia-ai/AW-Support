# GlassBuild 2024 - A+W Software GmbH

A+W’s 45+ years of expertise and dedication to customer satisfaction deliver innovative software solutions for glass, window, and door manufacturers. Our custom-configured software ensures precision, efficiency, and reliability across your operations.

Explore A+W Clarity for flat glass solutions, offering a suite of intelligent solutions spanning order management, scanning with smartphones, e-commerce, CAD design, production optimization, dispatch, delivery, and more. Whether you’re a small-scale fabricator or a large-scale manufacturer, A+W Clarity software solutions serve as the “brain” of your company today and in the future.

 Discover A+W Cantor for window and door solutions, streamlining your e-commerce, scanning with smartphones, production, and technical processes with a fully integrated solution. From customization and manufacturing to delivery, A+W Cantor empowers window, door, and roller shutter manufacturers to excel in today’s competitive landscape.

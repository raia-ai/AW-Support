# Cooling Brothers - A+W Software GmbH

#### "With A+W Business, A+W provides an excellent and contemporary product to the international flat glass industry. The software shows the great potential of modern “WINDOWS” systems in terms of more features and ergonomic and user-friendly functions. Getting new users up to an excellent skill-level has become even easier!"

#### Christian Busch

##### \- Cooling Brothers Pty Ltd.

## Continuous improvement for unmatched customer service

Cooling Brothers has been successfully trading for over 40 years. With this experience the company is at the market’s forefront for quality, service and expert knowledge.

Cooling Brothers recognizes the value of customer satisfaction in a competitive world and the major contribution to this satisfaction is made by the supply of products to an assured level of quality. This is achieved by means of state of the art machinery and a comprehensive A+W software solution for both the office and the shop floor. A high degree of integrated software automation contributes to the best possible quality, e.g. -Clean and undamaged surfaces and coatings by avoiding manual handling.

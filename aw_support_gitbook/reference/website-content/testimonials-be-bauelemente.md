# BE Bauelemente - A+W Software GmbH

#### "With what we have created with A+W Cantor, we are the market leader when it comes to configurators."

#### Hartmut Pantel

##### \- BE Bauelemente GmbH

## Consistent digital processes from A – Z with A+W iQuote and A+W Cantor

BE Bauelemente GmbH is one of the leading companies in the window & door industry. With its BE Windows & Doors brand, it sets standards in quality and reliability – for dealers and end customers alike.

In 2006, BE Bauelemente GmbH, a Schweiker Group company, took over the Schüco locations in Leopoldshöhe and Großkugel for the production and sales of BE Windows & Doors. From the very beginning, BE has worked consistently with A+W Cantor software in administration and production.

And BE, as an innovative company, is moving further in the direction of digitization. Not only in production, but also in the commercial area, digital, end-to-end processes are crucial. For example, digital sales channels shorten the path to the customer and create real added value for all partners involved.

# Digitalize Your Stock with A+W Smart Companion

Discover our innovative digitalization solution and revolutionize your stock with our new **A+W Smart Companion** **Stock**.  
With A+W Smart Companion Stock, you can monitor all processes in your stock – from incoming goods to stock movements and inventory to stock removals.

![Iphone Smart Companion](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/iPhone-SMC_2023.png "iPhone-SMC_2023")

## Exploit your full potential

![01 no paper 1](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/01_no-paper-1.png "Smart Companion Stock 2023 1")

Consume less paper and use current data from your A+W system right away. Record and process data right where it is created.

![02 time re](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/02_time_re.png "Smart Companion Stock 2023 2")

Say goodbye to unnecessary steps and duplicate entries. Focus on the essentials and profit from your companion.

![03 digital only cut](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/03_digital-only_cut.png "Smart Companion Stock 2023 3")

Enough of annoying sources of errors such as switched digits and forgotten numbers. Eliminate cumbersome workflows and rely on digitalized processes. 

## Seamless integration into your work processes

Tackle tasks on-site and without tiresome data procurement at fixed stations in production. The best: Your people remain focused on their tasks, and nothing gets lost.

Here’s an example from incoming goods: ordered TGs for your IG production. Thanks to the easy incoming goods registration in A+W Smart Companion at the loading ramp, all your colleagues know what’s what in real time. This way, you can plan the batch with the urgently required ordered lites and put it right into production. Furthermore, the lites can be booked to a PDC rack by the incoming goods registration in A+W Smart Companion Stock. In addition, you can print your own labels with their barcode and trace the lite seamlessly in your production. And all this thanks to a single action in your A+W Smart Companion Stock! 

This is one of many examples of the workflow with ordered lites in your production. Of course, the A+W Smart Companion Stock handles all types of incoming and outgoing goods registration, whether fittings, frame parts, sealing compound or your employees’ PPE, thus providing a central solution for all warehousing processes. A great advantage for you is the resulting, constant inventory in your Digital Warehouse 4.0.

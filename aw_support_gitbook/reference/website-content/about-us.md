# A+W Software is Customer Success - Sustainability

## About us

## Your Reliable Partner

### A+W Software: Innovating Since 1977

Established in 1977, A+W Software is a trailblazer in developing innovative, comprehensive, and modular software solutions for fabricators in the flat glass, window, and door industry. Our software solutions, designed with your commercial and production needs in mind, play a pivotal role in achieving sustained process optimization within your business and throughout the industry.

### Leading Software Solutions for the Flat Glass, Window, and Door Industry

Positioned as the software leader for the flat glass, window, and door industry, we relentlessly strive to develop ERP software, PPS software, optimization software, and innovation software solutions that address your challenges and enhance your company’s efficiency. Irrespective of your company’s size, we’re focused on crafting solutions that are custom configured (not custom programmed) to meet your specific needs and help your business grow and thrive.

### 45+ Years of Experience and 1100+ Satisfied Customers

Drawing on over 45 years of experience and serving more than 1100 customers globally, our team has encountered, navigated, and solved practically every challenge you might face. Combined with our extensive cooperation and communication with hundreds of machine manufacturers, we provide optimal solutions tailored precisely for your business. These tailored solutions empower you to oversee and optimize the complete value chain from order entry to delivery for flat glass, as well as finished windows and doors.

### Harness Our Knowledge for Your Business Growth

*   Over 1100 customers worldwide
*   Approximately 200 employees worldwide
*   Roughly 50 employees in Research & Development

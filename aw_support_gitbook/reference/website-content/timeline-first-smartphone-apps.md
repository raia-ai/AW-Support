# First smartphone apps - A+W Software GmbH

by | Jan 11, 2024

![](https://www.a-w.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Quer_AW-Smartphone-Apps-1080x675.webp)

In 2018, A+W took another big step in our digital transformation by introducing our first smartphone apps (A+W iShape & A+W Smart Companion). These apps were specifically designed to improve and speed up order entry and tracking. By using the latest technology, our customers were now able to enter orders directly from their mobile devices and track them in real time.

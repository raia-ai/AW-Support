3.2. Presentation
This module offers the possibility to use QR barcodes in Crystal Reports. They can be printed on
different reports. The module includes the necessary Windows fonts including the license to use
them and the integration into Crystal Reports.


3.3. Requirements
The minimum requirement is the SAP Crystal Reports Runtime (article 785014). The Crystal Report
Designer (article 785016 or 785012) is only required if you want to create reports yourself.


3.4. List of Functions
This module offers the possibility to use QR barcodes in Crystal Reports and to print them on
suitable printers.
Not included in this module is the creation of such reports.


3.5. Limitations
This module only supports the output of QR barcodes on reports created with SAP Crystal
Reports. The content of the QR barcodes should not exceed 800 characters.
Not included in this module is the use (i.e. reading or evaluation) of the QR barcodes in the
current A+W products, such as:
- A+W Smart Companion
- A+W Production Terminals

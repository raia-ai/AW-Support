# Quotations Tutorial

---
## Optimization of Quotations

In case of uncommon sheet sizes or quantities, you can pass a quotation to production as a trial in order to calculate the waste. The reported data can be used to recalculate the prices for the quotation.

The optimization of quotations is handled in the training module on Production.

### List of Alternative Quotations

All alternative quotations for a quotation are listed. Tick an alternative to show the details.

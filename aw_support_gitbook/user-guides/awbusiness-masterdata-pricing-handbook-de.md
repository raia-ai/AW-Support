long_description: "This document is a detailed software reference guide for the \"Preise\" (Pricing) section of the A+W Business Stammdaten (Master Data) module. It provides step-by-step instructions and explanations for managing various pricing-related functionalities. Key topics covered include copying and modifying prices for exchange articles, deleting exchange prices, managing price groups, assigning products to price groups, handling group surcharges, and setting up special pricing structures like insurance prices and mixed calculations. The manual details specific dialogs, registers (tabs), and fields within the software, complete with screenshots for visual reference. It explains concepts such as price rounding, absolute vs. percentage-based surcharges/discounts, and price specifications for different product types (e.g., ISO, VSG glass). The guide is intended for system administrators and power users responsible for configuring and maintaining the pricing logic within the A+W Business ERP system."

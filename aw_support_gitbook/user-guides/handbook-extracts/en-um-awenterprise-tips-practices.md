## Tip 32

                      Invoices
                      Invoicing is based on the terms of delivery and payment defined in the order.
                      Partial or collective invoices can be agreed which are usually issued after the
                      goods have been shipped. Once issued, an invoice cannot be corrected.
                      Price corrections are possible only by issuing a credit note, or sending an ad-
                      ditional invoice. Apart from complete, partial, and collective invoices, cash sale
                      invoices serve to enter order, delivery note, and invoice simultaneously; this is
                      a useful function for cash sales. Under certain circumstances, glass can be in-
                      voiced from production to trade.

## Tip 52


              Article master data describe all products. Every article is described by a variety
              of features and properties. This includes the product name, article type, prod-

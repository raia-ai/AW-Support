The guide is structured into two main tutorials and a software reference section. Tutorial 1 introduces basic ideas and covers the setup of customer data, supplier data, product data, price master data, surcharges, and discounts. It explains the core concepts and provides step-by-step instructions for entering and maintaining this information. Tutorial 2 builds upon the first, focusing on documents, invoicing settings, text and document printing, and other advanced configurations. The document also includes a detailed 'Revision Overview' tracking changes across different software versions, and an extensive 'Software Reference' section that provides in-depth information on various modules and settings, including general configurations, product classes, partner management, pricing structures, and system settings. It is intended for users responsible for entering and maintaining master data, requiring familiarity with the flat glass industry's terminology and basic commercial processes."

# A+W Business

---
## Add-ons
This section covers the available add-ons for the A+W Business suite.

### A+W CAD Designer - Bars
**Features**
- Take care of individually designed products.
- Easy and quick creation and dimensioning of complex grill pattern designs with graphical tools and modern user guidance.
- Direct control of the muntin saw possible.
- Cost and time savings thanks to generation and use of templates.
- Auxiliary points for free patterns.
- Drill-hole symmetrical or field symmetrical construction.

### A+W CAD Designer - Shapes
**Features**
- Easy and quick creation of complex individual shapes and standard template files for re-use in ERP systems.
- Easy entering and changing of difficult shapes and processings (e.g., edge deletion or complex cut-outs).
- Possibility of direct control of your CNC machine via DXF transfer.
- Extension of your product-specific shape catalogue by free constructions with A+W CAD Designer (Shapes).
- Import and processing of DXF-files.

### A+W EDI Import & Export
**Features**
- By means of the EDI interface, customer orders can be automatically imported into the order system.
- Purchase orders can be electronically transmitted to suppliers.
- The ERP system at the customer's or supplier's end must be capable of reading and filling the A+W EDI interface.

### A+W Fittings Catalogue Connector
**Features**
- **Fittings Catalogue Connector (Base Data Import)**
    - Master data of selected door and fitting manufacturers can be imported (Dorma, Pauli, Bohle, Velux, Roto, Wolff, SWS, BET2000, Schlechtendahl).
    - Depending on the interface type, product numbers, descriptions, and prices are imported.
- **Fittings Catalogue Connector (B2B Ordering)**
    - Purchase orders for the fitting manufacturer (Dorma) can be handled automatically.

**Specs**
- BME CAT from fitting supplier is needed.

### A+W Document Management Connector
**Features**
- This module enables archiving of printed commercial documents sent by mail services into selected archiving systems (Saperion, ELO ...).
- Depending on the archiving system, archived documents are provided with internal control and indexing information or additional index files.
- *Image Description: A flowchart shows documents (Extern: AB, RG, LS, sonstiges) from A+W Business being processed. They can go through "Alternativ ODBC" or directly as TIF/JPG/Druckjob with an Indexfile to an archiving system like Actiware, Sharepoint, or ELO.*

### A+W Gantry Interface
**Features**
- Synchronization of the stock balances of the A+W ERP Inventory systems and Hegla or Bystronic inventory systems.
- Stock entries and exits are exchanged, and stock balances can be automatically aligned on a periodical basis.
- Up-to-date overview of inventories in the portal storage from within the ERP stock system.
- The online connection enables continuously integrated processes from the order to consumption of glass in production.
- No time-consuming manual inventory or stock correction in the ERP system is required.

### Production Manager
**Features**
- Fast and automated optimization of orders and quotes, also for cost and glass needs.
- Create production jobs and optimization with human sensitivity.
- Selection of required production papers.
- Manual fine tuning of optimization parameters, adding of breakages and automatic use of residual.
- Optimization with different strategies and selection of the best for your company each time.
- System calcifies your products quickly in production flows to increase your productivity.

### Breakout Display
**Features**
- Fast and easy to use breakout display for the cutting table.
- Customizable information to be displayed.
- Compare different optimization and select the best one for your company.
- Automatic printing of labels for glass and residual plates at the cutting table.
- Report breakages.

### Residual Plate Management
**Features**
- Automatic use and storage of Residual plates.
- Automatic update of inventory of residual plates by system after cutting.
- Flexible priority of residual plates in cutting.
- Select the best residual plate for your breakages.

### A+W Capacity Planner
**Features**
- Control of delivery date based on machine loads and lead times.
- Visualization of machine loads.
- Identify bottlenecks and free capacity in your production.
- Cost calculation based on production time.
- Identify not finished work, backlogs.

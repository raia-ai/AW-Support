## Processes with A+W Software for Glass

### A+W Clarity Flowchart

This diagram shows the integrated processes managed by A+W Clarity for glass manufacturing.

- **Customer & Order Management:**
    - Web shop for glass
    - Order, P.O. and quotation management (integrates with openTRANS, EDI, XML)
    - Complaint management
    - Invoicing (connects to External FinAc)

- **Planning & Procurement:**
    - Material procurement
    - Stock management
    - Tech. analysis and production scheduling
    - Production planning (capacity planning)

- **Production & Control:**
    - Work preparation, rack allocation, yield optimization
    - Cutting
    - Glass processing and assembly
    - Production control and monitoring
    - Machine communication control, barcoding and monitoring

- **Logistics & Delivery:**
    - Delivery and dispatch planning
    - Glass delivery
    - Delivery monitoring

- **Overarching Systems:**
    - **Statistics and Business Intelligence:** Interfacing with all other processes and steps.
    - **Industry 4.0/IoT and Document Management:** Interfacing with all other processes and steps.

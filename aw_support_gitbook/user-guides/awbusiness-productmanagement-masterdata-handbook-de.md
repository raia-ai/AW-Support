long_description: "This document is a comprehensive software reference guide for the A+W Business application, specifically detailing the 'Stammdaten' (Master Data) module related to products. It provides step-by-step instructions and explanations for managing all aspects of a product's lifecycle within the system. Key areas covered include: 'Produktverwaltung' (Product Management): How to create, edit, and manage product master data, including articles, bills of materials (BOMs), and product variants. 'Klassifikatoren' (Classifiers): Setting up and assigning classifiers to products for better organization and searchability. 'Preis/Zuschlag' (Pricing/Surcharges): Configuring pricing structures, including price lists, surcharges for special features (like models or specific dimensions), and exchange surcharges for ISO or VSG glass. 'Fertigung' (Manufacturing): Defining manufacturing-specific parameters such as edge quality, structure orientation, and production-related keys for integration with A+W Production systems. 'Lager/Einkauf' (Inventory/Purchasing): Managing inventory parameters, procurement types, and integration data for systems like SAP. 'Technische Parameter' (Technical Parameters): Entering and managing technical specifications for glass products, such as Ug-values, g-values, sound insulation, and tolerances. 'Spezialfunktionen' (Special Functions): Detailed instructions on handling special cases like spacer restrictions, processing restrictions, and creating iTOE rules for automated order entry from CAD systems. The manual is intended for system administrators and power users responsible for maintaining the product master data, ensuring data accuracy for sales, purchasing, manufacturing, and financial processes. It includes screenshots, examples, and cross-references to other relevant sections and tutorials within the A+W Business documentation."

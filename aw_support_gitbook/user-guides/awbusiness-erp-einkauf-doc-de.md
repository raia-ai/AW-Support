long_description: "This document provides a comprehensive technical reference for the A+W Business software, focusing on the Sales (\"Verkauf\") and Purchasing (\"Einkauf\") modules. It is structured as a user guide and tutorial, explaining how to manage various business processes and documents such as orders, credit notes, and invoices. Key sections include: **FiBu-Übergabe** (Financial Accounting Transfer), detailing the configuration for exporting financial data; **Import/Export von Zahlungen**, which covers handling payment information, particularly for Brazilian banking standards; **Übergabe Archiv**, explaining the process of archiving documents and managing statistics; **Objektabrechnung** (Project Accounting), which describes the management and billing of projects; and various reporting and analysis tools like **Preisänderung (Report)** and **Deckungsbeitrags-Analyse** (Contribution Margin Analysis). The purchasing section covers the entire procurement lifecycle from creating purchase requisitions and orders, managing supplier confirmations (AB Lieferant), processing goods receipts (Wareneingang), to invoice verification (Rechnungskontrolle). The manual is intended for end-users and administrators, providing step-by-step instructions, descriptions of user interface dialogs, and explanations of underlying concepts and required master data configurations."

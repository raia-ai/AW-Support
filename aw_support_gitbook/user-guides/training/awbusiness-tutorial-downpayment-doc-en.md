## Down Payments

4.  Select the type of payment (B): **Down payment** or **Partial payment**.
    The payment amount can be entered as a down payment or partial payment. In the case of a partial payment, you assume that the amount will be paid in the form of installments.
5.  Select the payment method, e. g. transfer.
6.  Enter the down payment amount (C) and check the currency.
7.  Add remarks, e. g. the booking number of the bank.
8.  Click on [New] to save the down payment.
